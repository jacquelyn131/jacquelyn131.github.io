FAQ

Why does she draw eyes like that?

Jacquelyn Shelby is a self taught artist who learned while she was studying abroad in China. She learned from how-to-draw books from different bookstores which were mostly by Japanese or Korean artists. That's how the books told her to draw eyes so that's how she learned to draw eyes when she was first learning. ^_^
